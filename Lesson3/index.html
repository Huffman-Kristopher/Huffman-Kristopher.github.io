Purpose

The Primary purpose of this site is to deliver reliable and accurate weather forecasts for individuals so they can plan their day and any activities they may have outdoors. The secondary purpose for this site is for extended planning for the user. The user can see tomorrow and 10day forecast options, this will allow for the user to get a better idea of the future weather conditions.
