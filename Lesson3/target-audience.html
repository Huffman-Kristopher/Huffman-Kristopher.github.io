Target Audience

Who: People interested in weather conditions in their area. Age: 10 - 120 Motivation: Wants to do something outdoors or plan for weather conditions.


Persona One

Age: 38 Occupation: Adventurer

I'm Meg Huffman. I am an adventurer and explorer. I write books about the different places to visit around the world that are hidden gems from the main stream of tourists. I use journals to catalog my trips and compile my notes for my books. I like to do my exploring on sunny days if I can, so I check the weather on my phone multiple time a day.

Persona Two

Age: 5 Occupation: Kindergarten Student

I'm Landon Huffman. I am Kid and like to play out side. My Mom and Dad only let me play outside on nice days. I check the weather each morning to see if I can go outside and play.
